# Weight Initialization 

## Goal of Weight Initialization

Weights should:

1. Be **small in value** (avoid explosion)
2. Have **good variance** (avoid vanishing)
3. Be **random (not same)** (break symmetry)

---

##  Basic Setup

* Number of input units = `n_in`
* Number of output units = `n_out`

---

##  1. Simple Uniform Initialization (Basic)

Weights are initialized as:

$$
W \sim \mathcal{U}\left(-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n_{in}}}, ; \frac{1}{\sqrt{n_{in}}}\right)
$$

👉 Keeps values small, but **does not fully control variance across layers**

---

##  2. Xavier Initialization (Glorot)

###  Use for:

* Sigmoid
* Tanh

---

###  Xavier Normal
$$
W \sim \mathcal{N}\left(0, \frac{2}{n_{in} + n_{out}}\right)
$$
---

###  Xavier Uniform
$$
W \sim \mathcal{U}\left(-\sqrt{\frac{6}{n_{in}+n_{out}}}, ; \sqrt{\frac{6}{n_{in}+n_{out}}}\right)
$$
---

### 💡 Key Idea

* Balances variance between **forward pass** and **backprop**
* Prevents vanishing gradients in sigmoid/tanh

---

##  3. He Initialization (Kaiming)

### Use for:

* ReLU (most important in practice)

---

###  He Normal
$$
W \sim \mathcal{N}\left(0, \frac{2}{n_{in}}\right)
$$
---

###  He Uniform
$$
W \sim \mathcal{U}\left(-\sqrt{\frac{6}{n_{in}}}, ; \sqrt{\frac{6}{n_{in}}}\right)
$$
---

### 💡 Key Idea

* ReLU kills ~50% of neurons (negative → 0)
* He initialization **boosts variance** to compensate

---

##  Quick Comparison

| Method        | Distribution   | Formula Uses         | Best For      |
| ------------- | -------------- | -------------------- | ------------- |
| Basic Uniform | Uniform        | ( n_{in} )           | Simple models |
| Xavier        | Normal/Uniform | ( n_{in} + n_{out} ) | Sigmoid, Tanh |
| He            | Normal/Uniform | ( n_{in} )           | ReLU          |

---

##  Final Intuition 

* Initialization controls **signal flow**
* Goal:
  👉 Keep activations and gradients **stable across layers**

If:

* Too large → **exploding gradients**
* Too small → **vanishing gradients**

---

##  Insight

At your level, don’t just memorize formulas. Anchor this:

> Xavier = balance both sides
> He = fix ReLU damage

That alone is enough to recall everything correctly under pressure.
